About Aneeb Chemicals

A decade of chemical innovation, rooted in India, built for the world.

Our Story

Building the Future with Chemistry

Founded in 2014, Aneeb Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. started with a clear mission: to deliver innovative, cost-efficient, and sustainable construction chemical solutions that make Indian infrastructure stronger and smarter.

From a 10,000 sq. m. state-of-the-art facility at the outskirts of Delhi-NCR, we manufacture a comprehensive range of waterproofing compounds, adhesive systems, construction chemicals, tile-fixing solutions, and laminated glass inter-layers — all developed with deep technical expertise and rigorous quality control.

Our flagship Aneeb GCF Glass Solution represents a proud milestone: an indigenously developed photo cross-linked inter-layer, created in partnership with CCSU's Chemistry Department and DST (Government of India) under the Make in India initiative, using UV-curing technology that is both more energy-efficient and environmentally responsible than conventional methods.

Our Journey

2014
Company Founded Aneeb Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. incorporated in Ghaziabad with focus on construction chemical solutions.
2016
Product Range Expansion Launched the full Waterproofing & Tile Fixing solutions line. Achieved first major bulk supply contracts in Northern India.
2019
R&D Partnership Partnered with CCSU Chemistry Department and DST, Govt. of India to develop UV-cured laminated glass technology.
2022
Aneeb GCF Launch Commercially launched Aneeb GCF Laminated Glass Solution — the first indigenous UV-cured inter-layer in India.
2025
10,000 sq.m. Facility Expanded manufacturing to full capacity with advanced quality testing labs and pan-India distribution.
