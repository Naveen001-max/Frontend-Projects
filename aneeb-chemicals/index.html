Who We Are

Innovation-Driven Chemical Solutions Since 2014

Aneeb Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. delivers cost-efficient, sustainable construction solutions across waterproofing, adhesives, tile fixing, plastering, and laminated glass. From a 10,000 sq. m. facility on the outskirts of Delhi-NCR, our products serve construction, chemical, mining, and infrastructure sectors.

Our flagship Aneeb GCF Glass Solution is an indigenous, photo cross-linked inter-layer developed under the Make in India initiative in collaboration with CCSU's Chemistry Department and the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India.
